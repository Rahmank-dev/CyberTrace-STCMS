Who We Are

CyberTrace is India's leading cyber-fraud reporting and case-tracking platform designed to bridge the gap between victims and investigators. Our mission is to make cybercrime reporting fast, secure, and transparent.

With cybercrime rising 300% in India, we provide a trusted digital channel where citizens can report fraud instantly and track investigation progress in real-time. 10,000+ cases solved, 98% success rate.
